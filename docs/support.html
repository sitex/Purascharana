Purascharana - Mantra Counter

How to Use

Tap the counter to increment your mantra count. Use the settings to configure your target and preferences.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data backed up?

Your counter data is stored locally on your device. It will be included in your iCloud backup if you have iCloud backup enabled.

How do I reset my counter?

You can reset your counter from the Settings screen within the app.
