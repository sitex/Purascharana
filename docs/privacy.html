Privacy Policy

Purascharana

Last updated: December 9, 2024

Overview

Purascharana is a simple mantra counter app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal data. All data (your counter values and settings) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Storage

Your counter data is stored only on your device using the system's local storage. If you delete the app, this data is also deleted.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can contact us through the app's App Store page.
